DinkUp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Email address — used for account creation and authentication
  • Display name and username — shown to other players in the app
  • Skill level — used to match you with appropriate games
  • Location — used to show nearby pickleball courts (only when app is in use)
  • Game history — wins, losses, and game participation records
  • Profile photo — optional, displayed to crew members

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To organize and schedule pickleball games
  • To display leaderboards and game statistics
  • To show nearby courts based on your location
  • To send login verification codes via email
  • To enable crew collaboration features

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users — your display name, username, and game stats are visible to other DinkUp users
  • Service providers — we use Supabase for data storage and Resend for email delivery
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our rights

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Game history older than one year may be automatically deleted. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, visit our account deletion page or email us at joshrunner85@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

DinkUp is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
