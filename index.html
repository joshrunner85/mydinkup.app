Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

What we collect

DinkUp collects your email address for sign-in, and any profile information you provide (display name, skill level, profile photo). We also collect game and crew data you create within the app.

How we use it

Your information is used solely to operate DinkUp — scheduling games, managing crews, and sharing game invites. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data storage

All data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure in the United States. Profile photos are stored in Supabase Storage. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Location

If you grant location permission, DinkUp uses your location to show nearby courts on the map. Location is only accessed while the app is in use and is never stored on our servers.

Photos and camera

Camera and photo library access is only used when you choose to set a profile photo or crew image. Photos are uploaded to your account storage and are not shared with other users except as part of your visible profile.

Calendar

Calendar access is only used when you explicitly add a game to your calendar. We do not read your existing calendar events.

Push notifications

If you grant notification permission, DinkUp sends you alerts for game updates — when your game fills up, when a player joins, or when a game reminder fires. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Account screen in the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, game history, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at privacy@mydinkup.app.
